**** Temple City Of India **********

Bhubaneswar is the capital of the Indian state of Odisha, located in the Khordha district. The region, especially the old town, was historically often depicted as Chakra Khetra and Ekamra Khetra (Area adorned with a mango tree). Bhubaneswar is dubbed the "Temple City", a nickname earned because of many temples which are standing there.
